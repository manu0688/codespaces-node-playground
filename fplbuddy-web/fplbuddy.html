ℹ️ 🃏 How are chip recommendations made?▾
  • Wildcard — GW with highest density of easy fixtures across top FPL teams
  • Free Hit — GW with the most blanks or a high-value double gameweek reset opportunity
  • Bench Boost — GW where the most teams have a double gameweek so bench players also score
  • Triple Captain — GW where the best premium player has the easiest single fixture
  • Scores — Each GW gets a composite score; highest score = recommended GW to play that chip
ℹ️ 💰 How is price change predicted?▾
  • Transfer velocity — Heavy net buying relative to ownership signals a likely price rise
  • FPL's rule — A player rises when net transfers exceed ~1% of total managers owning them
  • ep_next — High expected points + high net transfers = strong rise candidate
  • Price fall signal — Heavy net sell-off with low form triggers a fall flag
  • Note — These are signals, not guarantees. FPL processes prices once per day after 6am BST
